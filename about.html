Who We are?

Sam is a standard Seven pupil at Nyanza Primary School. He lives with his parents at Mawingu Village and likes to play near the river with his donkey. Although his father Warned him not to play near the river several times, he didn’t listen. One day after school, he went to play with his donkey at the river. When he reached there, it started raining. Before the rain stopped, he tried to cross the river while seating on his donkey’s back but the water speed couldn’t let him do it.

After the rain had stopped, the good Samaritan took him back home, Sam asked for forgiveness from his father
